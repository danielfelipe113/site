Latest Projects

I'd like to share with you a personal journey that has led me to a new chapter in my life. As a front-end developer for the past eight years, I've spent countless hours working on projects for learning purposes. However, I've often found myself abandoning these projects halfway or never even publishing them at all. But, as they say, change is the only constant in life, and I'm excited to announce that I'm embracing this change.

After reflecting on my journey, I realized that my fear of failure and judgment often held me back from sharing my creations with the world. However, I've come to understand that showcasing my projects not only helps me grow as a developer but also allows me to connect with like-minded individuals who can provide valuable feedback and insights.

The following projects are a testament to my newfound commitment to publishing my personal projects. I'm eager to share this game with you all, and I hope they brings you as much joy and entertainment as they did to me while developing them. Your feedback and thoughts are highly appreciated, so don't hesitate to reach out!

In conclusion, I encourage all developers and creators to share their work with the world, regardless of the obstacles that might be holding you back. There is immense value in learning from one another, and together, we can continue to grow and evolve in our fields. After all, a wise person once said, "The only way to do great work is to love what you do."

Thank you for reading, and I hope you enjoy the projects! Let's bounce into this exciting new chapter together.

ChatBot - WhatsApp and ChatGPT

Built a ChatBot that utilizes the WhatsApp Business API to receive messages from users. Once received, a Firebase cloud function processes the message and sends it to Chat GPT, an advanced language model, before returning the response to the user. The cloud function also integrates with Firestore to store session messages, which are automatically deleted after a period of inactivity (10 minutes). My ChatBot aims to streamline communication with users and provide fast, personalized responses using state-of-the-art AI technology.
