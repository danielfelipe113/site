Passions

What is life without passions? Throughout the years, I have explored and pursued numerous interests, only to leave them behind. However, there are a few that have remained with me, and I would like to share them with you.

Painting

It was during a challenging period last year that I rediscovered the power of art. Holding a pencil in my hand, I contemplated drawing or coloring for the first time since my primary school days. Uncertain of where to begin, I embarked on a session and discovered that the pain I was experiencing faded away, replaced by a newfound focus on the pencil and paper before me. From that moment, I made the decision to embrace painting as a part of my life, and it has become a source of immense happiness and fulfillment.
